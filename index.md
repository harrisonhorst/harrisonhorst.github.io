# Harrison Horst 
Data Analyst, Washington, D.C. 

## About Me
Harrison is an early-career data analyst who specializes in the analysis of public data, combining his years of GIS mapping 
experience with intimate knowledge of census and governmental data. Most recently, Harrison worked as a Community Data Analyst
at IFF, a Midwest-based Community Development Financial Institution (CDFI), where he contributed to education landscape studies 
and risk & monitoring assessments. Before starting at IFF, Harrison worked on asset mapping research projects at a local sustainability 
non-profit and completed an M.A. in Sustainable Urban Development with a certificate in Sustainable Food Systems. He is passionate about 
community development, non-profit partnerships, data storytelling, and climate justice. Harrison also has qualifying skills in education
and communication with professional experience in ESL instruction, tutoring, journalism, and podcasting.
