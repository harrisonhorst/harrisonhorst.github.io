I am an early-career data analyst who specializes in the analysis of public data, combining years of GIS mapping 
experience with intimate knowledge of census and governmental data. Most recently, I worked as a Community Data Analyst
at IFF, a Midwest-based Community Development Financial Institution (CDFI), where I contributed to education landscape studies 
and risk & monitoring assessments. Before starting at IFF, I worked on asset mapping research projects at a local sustainability 
non-profit and completed an M.A. in Sustainable Urban Development with a certificate in Sustainable Food Systems. I'm passionate about community development, non-profit partnerships, data storytelling, and climate justice. I also have qualifying skills in communication with professional experience in ESL instruction, tutoring, journalism, and podcasting.
